Workshop on AI and Economics

April 13–14, 2026
Penn State University
Google Calendar Outlook Apple / iCal (.ics)

The AI and Economics Workshop brings together researchers to present and discuss recent work at the intersection of artificial intelligence and economics.

The inaugural edition will take place on April 13 and 14, 2026, at Penn State University.

Location

Penn State University

Penn State University is located in State College, Pennsylvania. Founded in 1855, the university and the town have developed alongside one another, forming the vibrant college community often known as "Happy Valley".

Getting to Penn State
The closest airport is University Park Airport (SCE), about 10 minutes from campus by car. It offers daily connecting flights through Philadelphia and Washington Dulles.

By car: ~3.5 hours from Washington, D.C., 3 hours from Philadelphia, 3.5 hours from Ithaca, and ~4.5 hours from New York City.
